In this essay, from chapter twelve: Speak Spanish, You’re in America!: El Huracán over Language and Culture, we’ll see a frequent problem in the United States of America. An issue that has been going on for centuries is the battle of languages between immigrants and Anglos. There is a difference of opinion between the Anglos wanting the immigrants to forget their native language and only speak English and whether the immigrants or Native Americans should be allowed to speak their native tongue without being discriminated against by others. We’ll also see how immigrants have significantly shaped the United States of America culturally.
